Our Services

ASSET TRADING & ASSET LEASING

We are experienced in the structuring, negotiation, and documentation of aircraft leasing and finance arrangements, including secured lending, operating leases, single-investor leases, leveraged leases, finance leases, cross-border leases, progress payment facilities, and warehouse facilities. We can help not only with original lease and financing transactions,including those involving multiple corporate or commercial aircraft and multiple leasing or finance parties.

OUTSOURCED GENERAL CONSULTING

We are also pleased to offer consulting and advisory services on corporate legal issues faced by aviation companies and various other commercial aviation related legal topics including, airline and Part 145 related matters, aviation insurance, cross-border transactional issues, etc

COMMERCIAL & CORPORATE TRANSACTIONS

We coordinate aircraft title searches, title transfers, ownership registrations, and lien searches and releases with the FAA and the International Registry. We also advise companies on the most favorable ownership or leasing structure to limit liability and minimize adverse tax consequences while complying with FAA regulations.
